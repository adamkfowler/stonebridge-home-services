How Our Service Works

We’ve simplified home maintenance to save you time and effort. Here’s how it works:

Step 1: Choose Your Plan

Select a subscription plan that fits your needs. Whether it’s monthly, bi-weekly, or weekly, we’ve got you covered. Check our Plans & Pricing page for more details.

Step 2: Tell Us What You Need

Use our easy-to-use portal to list the home maintenance tasks you need. You can also schedule a consultation if you're not sure what services you require.

Step 3: Relax While We Work

Your dedicated handyman will arrive on time and handle your tasks with professionalism and care. We ensure everything is done right, leaving your home in perfect condition.
