Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

We offer a wide range of handyman services including, but not limited to, general repairs, installations, and maintenance for both indoor and outdoor spaces.

Do you offer emergency services?

Yes, we provide emergency services for urgent repairs. Please contact us directly via phone for immediate assistance.

Can I subscribe to regular maintenance services?

Yes, we offer subscription plans that provide regular maintenance services at a discounted rate. Check our Plans & Pricing page for more details.

Are you licensed and insured?

While our services are designed to operate within the scope that does not require a contractor's license, we are fully insured for all the work we undertake.

How can I get an estimate?

You can receive an estimate by filling out the form on our Contact Us page or by calling us directly with details of your project.
